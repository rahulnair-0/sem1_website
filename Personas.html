Individuals I look up to..




Christian Bale

  Christian Bale is one of Hollywood's biggest star with a collection of critically-aclaimed and popular flims under his name.Playing protagonists
  as well as antagonists with equal poise and style, he is known for his role as the deranged psychopath in American Psycho(2001) as well as
  Bruce Wayne, the billionaire crime fighting super hero - Batman in the trilogy directed by Christopher Nolan.


Zayn

Zayn is a British singer who came to the limelight as one among the five singers in the world-famous band One Direction.
After breaking apart from the band, he chose to pursue his own style of music. His songs have a certain aesthetic
to them and the best part about him is that he enjoys what he does and the absence of greed for recognition through
awards and fame clearly reflects in his work.






Eminem

  Marshall Mathers better known by his professional name 'Eminem' is an American rapper,one of the most
  controversial yet best selling artists in the world.His lyrical genius and a mouth that runs faster than our legs
  continues to amaze his legion of fans who are popularly referred to as 'Stans'.





Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a business magnate, industrial designer, and engineer.He is the co-founder,CEO and product architect of
SpaceX and Tesla. THE "Tony Stark" of real life is a genius scientist and entrepreneur. He is a very jovial and one
of the favourite persons of the internet crowd because of his participation and appreciation of memes and viral trends.








Heath Ledger

  Heath Ledger was a Hollywood actor with incredible talent and taste for the art.He lives on forever in our hearts
  through his career best and invincible portrayal of the DC comic antagonist-The Joker. He won the Oscar award for the
  best supporting actor for the same. His acting prowess amazes us and makes us miss him all the more.
